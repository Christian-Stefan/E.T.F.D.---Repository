BESTOFRAC: Environmentally best practices and optimisation in hydraulic fracturing for shale gas/oil development

Project duration: 01.01.2017 - 31.12.2020 project support: EU: H2020-MSCA-RISE-2016

Abstract: This research brings together the complementary expertise of our consortium members to gain a better understanding of the physics in hydraulic fracturing (HF) with the final goal to optimize HF practices and to assess the environmental risks related to HF. This requires the development and implementation of reliable models for HF, scaled laboratory tests and available on-site data to validate these models. The key expertise in our consortium is on modelling and simulation of HF and all partners involved pursue different computational approaches. However, we have also some partners in our consortium which focus on scaled laboratory tests and one company which can provide on-site data.

Description: The final objective is to employ these models in order to answer some pressing questions related to environmental risks of HF practices, including
